About Us
ConvertIt is an interactive website that allows you to convert between different units of measurments. If you are traveling, doing homework or simply curious about different conversions, ConvertIt allows you to choose between: Weight: Pounds, Kilograms, Ounces, Grams and Tons Length: Millimeters, Centimerters, Inches, Feet, Yards, Meters, Kilometers and Miles Temperature: Celcius, Farenheit and Kelvin Time:Seconds, Minutes, Hours and Days Currency: US Dollars, Israeli Shekels, Euros, British Pounds and Canadian Dollars
Just chose an option from the Conversions dropdown to get started!
